Paranoma Rooftop Cafe - SEO Audit Report

We performed a comprehensive SEO audit on Paranoma Rooftop Cafe's website. Key improvements included optimizing meta tags, enhancing mobile responsiveness, and improving page load speed by 30%. We also fixed broken links and image alt tags, and implemented structured data markup for better search visibility.

Our structured performance roadmap focused on content quality, internal linking, and site architecture. These changes are projected to significantly increase organic traffic, improve Google ranking, and enhance user experience.
